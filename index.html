Dr. Jyotindra Narayan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna. His research interests include Rehabilitation Robotics, Human-Robot Interaction, and Control Systems. He completed his Ph.D. from IIT Guwahati, focusing on developing innovative solutions for rehabilitation robotics. Prior to joining IIT Patna, he held positions at Imperial College London and the University of Bayreuth, where he contributed to cutting-edge research in robotics and control systems.
