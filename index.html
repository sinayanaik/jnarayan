About SHRI Lab

Welcome to the Smart Healthcare & Rehabilitation Innovations Lab (SHRI Lab) under the direction of Dr. Jyotindra Narayan in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Patna. SHRI Lab focuses on developing cutting-edge robotic systems that assist people in rehabilitation and enable safe, natural human-robot interaction. Our research integrates advanced control systems, biomechanics, and machine learning to create innovative solutions for healthcare applications.

Dr. Jyotindra Narayan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna. His work focuses on developing robotic systems that assist people in rehabilitation and help humans interact safely and naturally with machines. He earned his Ph.D. from IIT Guwahati, where he developed innovative robotic solutions for movement recovery. Before joining IIT Patna, he worked at Imperial College London and the University of Bayreuth, where he contributed to advanced research in human-robot interaction and control.
